WHO ARE WE?

A micro team, with giant dreams.

CHARITY FOCUSED

We are Banaware, focused on giving charities and shelters the coverage and exposure they deserve. In a world where the bad word spreads like wildfire, we aim to give the good word an even greater power.

HAPPY FACES SAY A THOUSAND WORDS

How many times have you wanted to help, or talk to someone with a helping hand, but couldn't find the right person to do so?
"Too often" is what we always hear, and that's why we are here to create easy-to-reach online platforms, that encourage interactions between the general public and the charity/shelter workers.
By implementing FREE donations, everyone with a bit of spare time can watch an ad to give these small groups the support and love they deserve.
